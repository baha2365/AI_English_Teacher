📖 English Reading Task
My Wonderful Family
Reading Passage

Hi! My name is Sam. I am 12 years old. I live with my family in a small house. My family is very kind and happy.

My father is a teacher. He works at a school. My mother is a nurse. She works at a hospital. I have one sister and one brother. My sister is 10 years old, and my brother is 6 years old.

We like to spend time together. On weekends, we often watch movies or go to the park. Sometimes we cook dinner together. I love my family very much.

❓ Questions
1 How old is Sam?
2 What is Sam's father's job?
3 Where does Sam's mother work?
4 How many siblings does Sam have?
5 What do they often do on weekends?
⚠️ Please answer all 5 questions before confirming.
